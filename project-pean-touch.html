How We Strengthened Pean Touch Cleaning Services’ Brand Identity & Online Visibility Through Strategic Branding and PPC Advertising

Pean Touch Cleaning Services is a professional cleaning company offering residential and commercial cleaning solutions.

Project Description

Pean Touch Cleaning Services is a professional cleaning company offering residential and commercial cleaning solutions. As a growing service-based business operating in a competitive market, the brand needed to show up well online and position itself more professionally to attract higher-quality clients.

At the time of engagement, the business was starting out and didn’t have a strong and cohesive brand identity across its digital platforms. Their online presence wasn’t active and didn’t reflect the quality of their work or communicate their value effectively to potential customers.

Kadesh Digital was engaged to refine the brand’s visual identity, improve its online presence, and implement structured marketing strategies that would enhance visibility and drive service inquiries.

The Challenge

Pean Touch Cleaning Services was facing several challenges that limited its ability to grow consistently online:

  • Inconsistent brand visuals across digital platforms
  • Lack of a recognizable and professional brand identity
  • Limited online visibility
  • Difficulty standing out in a saturated cleaning services market
  • Low engagement on social media platforms
  • Minimal structured marketing efforts to attract new clients
  • Missed opportunities to convert online visibility into actual service inquiries

Without a strong brand presence or targeted digital campaigns, it was difficult for the business to effectively communicate credibility or attract the right audience.

The core challenge was:
How do we improve brand perception and increase service inquiries through strategic digital positioning?

The Solution

We implemented a comprehensive digital support plan focused on three key areas:

1. Branding & Visual Identity Development

We worked with the brand to develop a cohesive visual identity that aligned with its professionalism and service quality. This included refining brand elements to ensure consistency across all digital touchpoints such as:

  • Social media platforms
  • Marketing materials
  • Online communication assets

This helped establish a more recognizable and credible brand presence.

2. Social Media Management & Content Creation

To support visibility and audience engagement, we implemented a structured content strategy that focused on:

  • Service awareness posts
  • Before-and-after cleaning visuals
  • Educational and value-driven content
  • Brand storytelling
  • Promotional campaigns

Consistent publishing schedules and platform management helped strengthen the brand’s online presence and improve audience trust.

3. Targeted PPC Advertising Campaigns

We launched performance-driven PPC advertising campaigns aimed at:

  • Increasing brand visibility within relevant target locations
  • Reaching potential residential and commercial clients
  • Driving service inquiries

These campaigns were designed to move beyond awareness and generate actionable interest from potential customers.

The Result

Following the implementation of branding improvements and structured marketing campaigns, Pean Touch Cleaning Services experienced:

  • Improved brand perception across digital platforms
  • 150% Increased service inquiries
  • Stronger audience engagement
  • Enhanced online visibility within its target market
  • More consistent digital presence

The business was better positioned to communicate professionalism and attract clients who aligned with its service offerings.

Conclusion

By combining cohesive branding with structured content execution and targeted PPC advertising, we helped Pean Touch Cleaning Services strengthen its digital presence and improve how it was perceived by potential customers.

With a more consistent and credible online identity supported by performance-driven campaigns, the brand is now better equipped to attract and convert service inquiries within a competitive industry.

This project demonstrates the impact of aligning brand identity with intentional digital marketing strategies to support sustainable business growth.
