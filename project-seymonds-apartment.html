How We Increased Seymonds Apartment Bookings Through Strategic Video Marketing & Lifestyle Content

Project Description

Seymonds Apartment is a short-stay/Airbnb-style accommodation designed to offer guests a comfortable, stylish, and conveniently located living experience.

As competition within the short-term rental market continued to grow, the brand needed high-quality visual content that would not only showcase the apartment’s aesthetics but also communicate the experience of staying there.

At the time of engagement, Seymonds Apartment required professionally produced marketing videos that could:

  • Improve visibility online
  • Attract potential guests
  • Highlight the apartment’s proximity to key locations
  • Position the space as both aesthetically pleasing and experience-driven

Kadesh Digital was engaged to create compelling video content that would strengthen the property’s online appeal and contribute to increased bookings.

The Challenge

The short-stay and Airbnb market is highly visual and extremely competitive. Guests often make booking decisions based primarily on:

  • Visual appeal
  • Perceived comfort
  • Location advantages
  • Lifestyle experience

Seymonds Apartment faced the following challenges:

  • Limited high-quality marketing videos
  • Difficulty showcasing the full experience of staying at the apartment
  • The need to differentiate from similar listings
  • The need to communicate proximity to major locations effectively
  • A desire to increase inquiries and bookings through stronger visual storytelling

The core challenge was:
How do we create content that doesn’t just show the apartment but sells the experience?

The Solution

To address these challenges, we developed a two-part video marketing approach designed to highlight both aesthetics and lifestyle appeal.

1. Aesthetic Walkthrough & Location-Focused Video

We produced a professionally shot video that:

  • Highlighted the apartment’s interior design and layout
  • Captured the ambiance and comfort of the space
  • Showcased key features and amenities
  • Communicated its proximity to important locations and attractions

The goal was to help potential guests visualize the environment and understand its convenience.

2. Lifestyle Video Featuring a Micro-Influencer

To elevate the emotional appeal of the property, we created a second video focused on the guest experience.

This video featured a content creator/micro-influencer interacting naturally within the space — demonstrating:

  • Comfort
  • Relaxation
  • Lifestyle appeal
  • Real-life usability of the apartment

Instead of just presenting a static property, this approach helped position Seymonds Apartment as a place where guests could create enjoyable experiences.

3. Strategic Content Execution

Both videos were optimized for:

  • Social media visibility
  • Audience engagement
  • Platform-friendly formats
  • Short-form consumption

This ensured the content was not only visually strong but also aligned with digital viewing behavior.

The Result

Following the release of the marketing videos, Seymonds Apartment experienced:

  • Increased online visibility
  • Stronger audience engagement
  • Improved brand perception
  • More booking inquiries

The lifestyle-driven storytelling approach made the apartment more relatable and desirable to potential guests, contributing to increased interest and occupancy.

By combining aesthetic visuals with experiential storytelling, the property gained stronger appeal within a competitive rental market.

Conclusion

Through strategic videography and lifestyle-focused content, we helped Seymonds Apartment move beyond simple property display and into experience-driven marketing.

By showcasing not just the space, but the lifestyle it offers, the brand strengthened its online visibility and increased booking inquiries.

This project demonstrates the power of intentional visual storytelling in driving real business outcomes especially in industries where perception and presentation influence purchasing decisions.
